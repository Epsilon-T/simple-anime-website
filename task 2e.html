Kuroshitsuji-Kishuku

Kuroshitsuji: Kishuku is a Japanese animated film based on the popular manga and anime series Kuroshitsuji (Black Butler). The story follows the young Earl Ciel Phantomhive and his demon butler Sebastian as they embark on a new adventure in a mysterious boarding house. When strange occurrences begin to unfold, the duo must unravel dark secrets and confront supernatural forces while navigating themes of loyalty and vengeance. With its stunning animation and engaging plot, the film captures the essence of the original series while offering fans a fresh narrative.
